Introduction

PEW-PEW-PEW! is a side scrolling shooter game with various weapons and different enemies.The world of Dun was a peaceful one, filled with joy and prosperity. Then one day the people from the world on Tim attacked. The lives of the people of Dun and their kin are in your hands...

Blast through the enemies and rule the skies with your state of the art flying Doughnut equipped with the all new Coffee Bean shooter and a rocket launcher. Their fritters are small and quick but not strong enough to take more than one hit!

Genre: Side scrolling shooter

Platform: Desktop Web

Aesthetics: Light, pastel colours. I plan on drawing each of the 2D assets myself. I plan on using a scrolling background image

Gameplay: The player will use the mouse to move, left click to shoot bullets and right click for rockets
